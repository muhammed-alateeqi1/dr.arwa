## Mobile First (Highest Priority)

Assume that 95% of visitors will open this page by scanning a QR Code using their smartphone.

The mobile experience is MORE IMPORTANT than desktop.

The page must look incredible on:

iPhone

Samsung

Google Pixel

The first screen on mobile should immediately create a "WOW" feeling.

Avoid unnecessary scrolling.

The entire experience should comfortably fit inside one mobile viewport before reading the message.

Desktop should simply scale beautifully.


## Message Card

The message card should NOT be white.

Instead use a luxury black glass background.

Examples:

#111111

#151515

#181818

Use:

soft transparency

glass blur

thin gold border

very subtle green reflections

gold highlights

The message should be white.

Important words may use soft gold.

The card should feel like a premium invitation card.



## Typing Animation

Typing must feel realistic but NOT slow.

Target speed:

45–60 milliseconds per character.

Pause only:

250 ms after commas.

450 ms after periods.

700 ms before a new paragraph.

Never make the user wait unnecessarily.



## Opening Animation

Start with the company's green background.

The cartoon pineapple image slowly falls from the top.

Use realistic gravity.

Small rotation while falling.

Soft bounce when landing.

Tiny leaves movement.

After landing:

Golden glow appears.

Background lights activate.

The pineapple smiles through subtle motion (without changing the image).

Display:

"You unlocked something made especially for you."

Pause.

Then elegantly transition into the main page.

The cartoon pineapple exits gracefully.

The company logo fades into its final position.



## Brand Identity

The company logo should always feel present.

Not oversized.

Not distracting.

Use geometric pineapple outlines in the background inspired by the logo.

Everything should feel connected to the brand.



## Background

Use the official company green as the dominant color.

Create depth using:

blurred gradients

glass reflections

floating particles

subtle geometric pineapple outlines

soft golden light

The page should never look empty.

It should also never feel visually noisy.

## Final Objective

Do NOT build a webpage.

Do NOT build a landing page.

Build an emotional digital experience.

The recipient should feel that someone invested hours creating something unique just for them.

The first five seconds should create curiosity.

The next ten seconds should create emotion.

The final moment should leave a genuine smile.

Every animation, color, spacing, transition, typography choice, and interaction should contribute to that feeling.

The final result should be production-quality and visually comparable to premium experiences from Apple, Linear, Stripe, or Nothing.

Write code that another senior frontend developer would admire for its cleanliness and quality.
